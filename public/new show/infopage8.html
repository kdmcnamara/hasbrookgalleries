Bridgette Cookson

Oil Paint, Acrylic Paint, Tempera

About the Work :: “The art is about curiosity. It is a consideration of what is within? What is within our structures, and beyond our forms, the intangible essence, the anatomy of being. What is real? Tangible reality being illusion, looking through the material, past the constructs of time and culture, external influences to the ineffable and immortal within us all. Or something like that” -Bridgette Cookson
