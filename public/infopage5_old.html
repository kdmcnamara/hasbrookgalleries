Woman in Scarf & Sleeping Klimt

Isaac Lyons

Ink Marker and Graphite on Paper Bag Refuse

10.6”x6.7” & 11.2”x7.6”

About the Work :: While Lyons’ largest background is in aerosol paints, in this piece he delves into the human figure and their surroundings. Experimenting with color and form they create a very personal identity to each figure. The medium of choice is paper bag refuse, further playing into the sociological aspect of figurations.
